We are a trusted provider of sea freight services, offering reliable and cost-effective transportation solutions for businesses of all sizes. With our extensive experience in the industry, we are committed to providing our clients with comprehensive and hassle-free shipping services.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of sea freight services to handle your shipment needs. Our sea freight services include:

  • Full container load (FCL)
  • Less than container load (LCL)
  • Breakbulk or out of gauge
  • Temperature-controlled shipping
  • Project cargo
  • Customs clearance
  • Warehousing and distribution
  • Cargo insurance

At Meracus Global Services, we have a global network of partners and agents that allow us to provide seamless services across borders. Our team of experts will work with you to ensure that your cargo is transported safely and efficiently.

Technology and Tracking

We use state-of-the-art technology to monitor and track your shipment. Our web-based tracking system allows you to keep an eye on your shipment from start to finish, providing real-time information on departure, arrival, and other critical milestones. Additionally, our team will keep you informed throughout the entire shipping process, ensuring that you remain updated every step of the way.

Experienced and Professional Services

We understand that shipping cargo across the sea requires not only extensive expertise but also an in-depth understanding of regulations and customs compliance. That is why at Meracus Global Services, we have experienced staff who will advise you and ensure that your cargo meets all regulatory requirements. Our team of professionals will work with you to craft customized shipping solutions that meet your needs and budget.

If you are looking for a reliable and cost-effective sea freight provider that offers top-notch services, look no further than Meracus Global Services. Please feel free to contact us, and our team will be happy to answer any questions you may have and provide you with a custom quote that is right for your business.
